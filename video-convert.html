Video Format Guide

🎬
WebM (VP9) — Modern, open format by Google. Excellent compression with great quality. Best for web use and sharing online.
📹
WebM (VP8) — Older WebM codec. Wider browser compatibility, slightly larger files than VP9. Good fallback option.
🎞️
MP4 (H.264) — Most universally compatible format. Plays on virtually every device. Browser support for encoding varies.
🔒 Privacy: Video conversion uses your browser's built-in MediaRecorder API. No data leaves your device. Output format availability depends on browser support.
