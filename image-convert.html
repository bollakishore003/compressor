Which Format Should I Use?

📷
JPG — Best for photos. Small file sizes, no transparency. Ideal for sharing and web.
🎨
PNG — Best for graphics, logos, screenshots. Supports transparency. Larger than JPG.
⚡
WebP — Modern Google format. Smaller than JPG/PNG at same quality. Great for websites.
🎞️
GIF — Supports animation. Limited to 256 colors. Best for simple animated graphics.
